Speaking

  • Overall timing about 11-14 mins
  • Three parts
    • Introduction and interview: timing 4-5 mins
    • Long turn: 1 min to prepare for given task make notes before speaking 2-3 mins
    • Discussion: based on Part2 examiner 4-5 mins
  • Scoring
    • Fluency and coherence
    • Vocabulary
    • Grammar
    • Pronunciation
  • general tips
    • know what to do next and what examiners expect
    • natural language and answer quickly
